SMS opt-in flow

A record of the exact opt-in screen restaurant owners and managers see when enabling SMS alerts inside the Cavnar AI dashboard — provided for carrier/campaign review since that screen sits behind an authenticated login.

Product: Cavnar AI Restaurant Intelligence Dashboard (dashboard.cavnar.ai)
Where this appears: Account tab → Alert Settings → Delivery method
Who sees it: The restaurant's owner/manager, while configuring how they receive review and operational alerts for their own account

How opt-in works

Every Cavnar AI client account includes an Alert Settings screen where the restaurant owner or manager chooses how they want to be notified about new reviews, urgent activity (e.g. a 1-star or health-related review), and labor/inventory alerts. SMS is one of two delivery options (the other is email) and is off by default. To turn SMS on, the account holder must enter their own mobile number and affirmatively check a dedicated consent box before the setting can be saved — the consent checkbox is disabled and unchecked until SMS delivery is selected, and the save action is blocked entirely if SMS is selected without consent checked.
